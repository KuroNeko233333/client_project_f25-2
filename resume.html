Resume

Basics

  • One page for early career; clear sections.
  • Use action verbs; quantify impact.

Checklist

  • Consistent dates and formatting.
  • Keywords from the job description.
  • Keep for clarity.

Prepare for job search and Internships

  • Define 3–5 target role titles and the keywords.
  • Use LinkedIn/Handshake sites; apply regularly.
  • Have a resume and a short coverletter template ready.
  • Do 2–3 coffee chats per week; ask for advice.
  • Track applications for each posting.

Next steps

  • Share resume during networking
  • Align resume with your portfolio
